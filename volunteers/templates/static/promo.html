Volunteering

This is a site that brings all the volunteers together. Find all the cool tasks that you are interested in.

Problems? For bugs you can report an issue at Github.

  • FOSDEM

    FOSDEM is a free and non-commercial two-day event organised by volunteers to promote the widespread use of free and open source software. The goal is to provide free and open source software developers and communities a place to meet.

  • Documentation

    Task documentation for Volunteers can be found on doc.volunteers.fosdem.org.

  • Volunteering

    FOSDEM needs you! Every year, the FOSDEM team is assisted by an enthusiastic team of volunteers to help out with various tasks and make the event a fun and safe place for all our visitors.

  • Location

    FOSDEM 2026 will take place at the ULB in Brussels, Belgium, within many of the building on the Solbosch campus.

  • Stay Informed

    Join the Volunteering chat channel on Matrix: #fosdem-volunteers on matrix (webchat).

    You can also subscribe to receive updates from the mailing list.

  • Real People

    While you may have questions, please only reach out directly to the volunteer coordinators in case of a real emergency!

    You can find their contact information here on doc.volunteers.fosdem.org.
